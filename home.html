Dental Care

We welcome you to our dental care page

Endodontics

Diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and injuries of the dental pulp and tissues surrounding the tooth root.

Operative Dentistry

Diagnosis, prevention and minimally-invasive treatment of decay. Conservative correction of esthetic problems with dental bonding.

Family Dentistry

Diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and injuries of the dental pulp and tissues surrounding the tooth root

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

Grafting, dental implant placement and other surgical treatments of conditions, injuries, and esthetics of the teeth, jaws, and face

Oral Pathology, Radiology & Medicine

Investigation of clinical, radiographic, and microscopic features of oral / maxillofacial diseases and abnormalities

Orthodontics

Correction of irregularities of the teeth, bite, and jaw

Peadiatric Dentist

Oral health care for infants, children, and adolescents, including persons with special health care needs.

Periodontics

Treatment of the supporting dental structures, including gums and alveolar bone, and their replacements by grafting or implants.

Preventive & community Dentistry

Improvement of the public's oral health through prevention and control of oral diseases.

Prosthodontics

Complete oral rehabilitation, including the use of dentures, crowns, bridges, veneers, & implants.
